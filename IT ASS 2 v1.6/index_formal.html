Introduction

Nowadays, along with the development of smartphone technology, mobile cameras keep evoluting. They account for more and more proportion in the daily use of cameras. Thus, we decided to start the program to study and analyse the performance of the updated cameras. In this program, three types of mobile cameras will be introduced compared and ranked, through three different criteria. The criteria are functions, which are respectively Autofocus Detail and Zoom. Each Conclusion of the devices test are base on the opinions of DXO Mark.
